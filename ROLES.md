# Shikigami (式神) — Spirit Roles

Supernatural servants summoned by onmyōji (sorcerers) to do their bidding. They're commanded spirits that carry out tasks autonomously—a perfect metaphor for AI agents.

| Role            | Spirit        | Kanji  | Meaning                                                   |
| --------------- | ------------- | ------ | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Orchestrator    | **Onmyōji**   | 陰陽師 | The master who commands the shikigami                     |
| Work Agent      | **Shikigami** | 式神   | The summoned worker spirits                               |
| Review Agent    | **Tengu**     | 天狗   | Proud, critical mountain spirits known for their judgment |
| Test Agent      | **Kitsune**   | 狐     | Clever foxes who test and probe for weaknesses            |
| Failure Analyst | **Shinigami** | 死神   | Death god who examines what went wrong                    |
